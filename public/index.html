Overview

Fatebound is a visual novel experience with a few twists. While it uses the typical visual novel way of presenting scenes, it strays away from the norm by adding unique features and placing the emphasis differently. Unique additions are the exploration of a large overworld map, random events that spawn when criteria are met, and castle management elements. On the visual novel side, the game is inspired by the deep branched dialogue seen in Obsidian, Bioware, and the Witcher games. These dialogues are designed to be meaningful and often affect later events.

The game is set in The Six Realms, the westernmost continent in the world of Solanse. Seven years have passed since the last Demon War, and the defeat of Lord Karnas at the hands of the forces of Light ushered in a new era of peace and prosperity. Evil never lies dormant for long, and in the shadows, new threats to the tenuous peace begin to stir.

You play as Rowan, a veteran of the last Demon War. He and his fellow heroes, aided by the Goddess of Light, pursued Karnas to the heart of his stronghold, Castle Bloodmeen, and defeated him once and for all. Now living peacefully with his wife Alexia, Rowan is thrust back into conflict when a mysterious woman forces him into the service of the darkness he fought to destroy.

Core Features

While it uses the typical visual novel way of presenting scenes, it strays away from the norm by adding unique features and placing the emphasis differently. Unique additions are the exploration of a large overworld map, random events that spawn when criteria are met, and castle management elements.

The game also features castle management elements, allowing players to build and manage their own stronghold. This system is designed to add a layer of strategy to the otherwise narrative-driven gameplay.
